3. I don't use cap but this creation is just to pass the time. I need sunlight and rain to bath me. They are nature that I don't have any intention to hide much from it especially using cap. Therefore flower become my design to sew on it so probably it can grow from the sunlight and the rain to make me remember that I can never run away from nature. If you ask me what kind of flower is that? orchid? I don't know myself. I found it beautiful even so. "Flower on cap"

4. Home. Inheritance house is a prove that I am here today. I miss those time where my grandparents still alive. Al-fatihah to them. I was running in the the wooden floor make cracking sound. A lot of memories since then. It was memorable that I decidec to potray it in embroidery form. There will be always a story behind each embroidery I make.
